Breakdown!

SO! This will probably be second shortest page because of how easy and simple it is. It's so simple in fact, I could literally say, use a cleanser and moisturizer.... That's it. It's that easy! But we'll break it down since there's different skin types. Okay! There's three different types of skin for people: certain people have oily skin which produces a lot of oils and grease. There's people with dry skin, which means their skin doesn't produce enough oils. Then you have balanced skin which has a mix of both.


How to Cleanse!

'Kay! Let's break down how to cleanse and lets make this quick and easy, just to show how effortless this is.

  1. 1. WASH YOUR HANDS
  2. 2. Turn the sink to warm water
  3. 3. Start rubbing the warm water into your skin in order to open your pores for two minutes.
  4. 4. Depending on your cleanser, foaming hydrating for balanced or dry skin, and gel-based cleansers for oily skin, pour it into your palm about dime sized.
  5. 5. Start rubbing the cleanser with a bit of water mixed in before rubbing it onto your skin it circles, MAKE SURE ITS FOAMING.
  6. 6. After 3 minutes of rubbing, use cold water to wash it off.
  7. 7. Lastly, pat dry your face then use a light moisturizer to rub onto your face.

When!

Last part, when to cleanse? Well! Depending on your skin is how much you cleanse, if you have oily skin, it's ideal to cleanse twice a day. But if you have balanced or dry skin, once a day is just fine!
